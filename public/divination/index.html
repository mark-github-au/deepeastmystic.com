I Ching and Hexagrams

易经与卦象

The I Ching, or Book of Changes, is one of the oldest Chinese classical texts. Dating back over 3,000 years, it uses 64 hexagrams composed of broken and unbroken lines to represent the fundamental principles of reality.

Each hexagram consists of six stacked lines that can be either yang (unbroken) or yin (broken). Through the process of casting coins or yarrow stalks, a hexagram is formed that provides guidance and insight into your current situation and future possibilities.

Core Principles

  • Change: The only constant is transformation
  • Balance: Harmony between yin and yang
  • Timing: Understanding when to act or wait
  • Wisdom: Ancient guidance for modern decisions

Our practitioners use traditional methods combined with deep understanding of classical commentaries to provide accurate and meaningful readings for your life questions.

Zi Wei Dou Shu (Purple Star Astrology)

紫微斗数

Zi Wei Dou Shu, known as Purple Star Astrology, is one of the most respected forms of Chinese astrology. This sophisticated system maps out your life's journey through 12 palaces, each governing different aspects of your existence.

Using your birth date and time, we construct a detailed natal chart that reveals your personality, destiny, relationships, career path, health patterns, and wealth potential throughout different life stages.

The Twelve Palaces

  • Life Palace: Core personality and destiny
  • Siblings Palace: Family relationships
  • Spouse Palace: Marriage and partnerships
  • Children Palace: Offspring and creativity
  • Wealth Palace: Financial fortune
  • Health Palace: Physical wellbeing
  • Career Palace: Professional path
  • Property Palace: Real estate and assets

BaZi (Four Pillars of Destiny)

八字命理

BaZi, or Four Pillars of Destiny, is a comprehensive Chinese metaphysical system that analyzes the cosmic energies present at your moment of birth. Your birth chart consists of four pillars representing year, month, day, and hour, each composed of a Heavenly Stem and Earthly Branch.

This system reveals your elemental constitution, personality traits, fortune cycles, and how you interact with the world around you. The Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water) and their interactions provide deep insights into your life path.

What BaZi Reveals

  • Elemental Balance: Your five-element constitution
  • Life Cycles: 10-year fortune pillars (Da Yun)
  • Annual Fortune: Yearly energy influences
  • Career Aptitude: Suitable professions
  • Relationship Compatibility: Partnership dynamics
  • Health Indicators: Constitutional strengths and weaknesses
  • Wealth Timing: Optimal periods for financial growth

Tarot Card Reading

塔罗占卜

While Tarot originated in the West, it has found a harmonious place alongside Eastern divination practices at DeepEast Mystic. The 78-card Tarot deck, consisting of 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana cards, serves as a mirror to the soul and a guide through life's mysteries.

Each card carries symbolic imagery and archetypal meanings that speak to universal human experiences. Through intuitive reading and card spreads, we help you gain clarity on relationships, career decisions, spiritual growth, and life transitions.

Reading Approaches

  • Three-Card Spread: Past, Present, Future
  • Celtic Cross: Comprehensive life reading
  • Relationship Spread: Partnership insights
  • Career Path: Professional guidance
  • Year Ahead: Annual forecast
  • Decision Making: Clarity on choices

Our readers combine traditional Tarot wisdom with Eastern philosophical insights, creating a unique blend that honors both traditions while providing practical guidance for modern life.
