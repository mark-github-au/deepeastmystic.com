What is Feng Shui?

风水的本质

Feng Shui, literally meaning "wind-water," is an ancient Chinese practice that seeks to harmonize individuals with their surrounding environment. Dating back over 3,000 years, it combines art, science, and philosophy to optimize the flow of Qi (energy) in living and working spaces.

The practice examines how the placement of objects, buildings, and even entire cities can affect health, wealth, relationships, and overall fortune. By understanding the natural flow of energy and applying time-tested principles, we can create environments that support success and wellbeing.

Core Elements

  • Bagua (kf): Eight trigrams mapping life areas
  • Form School: Physical landscape and shapes
  • Compass School: Directional energies and time

Residential Feng Shui

住宅风水

Your home is your sanctuary, and its energy directly impacts your life quality. Residential Feng Shui analyzes your living space to optimize areas governing health, relationships, family harmony, and personal growth.

We examine the orientation of your home, room layouts, furniture placement, color schemes, and the interaction of the five elements. Each room serves different functions and requires specific energy balancing for optimal results.

Key Areas We Address

  • Main Door: The "mouth of Qi" - primary energy entrance
  • Master Bedroom: Health, relationships, and rest quality
  • Kitchen: Wealth and family nourishment
  • Living Room: Family harmony and social connections
  • Home Office: Career success and productivity
  • Children's Rooms: Education and development
  • Bathrooms: Preventing wealth drainage
  • Garden: External environment and energy flow

Commercial Feng Shui

商业风水

Business success requires more than hard work - the energy of your commercial space plays a crucial role. Commercial Feng Shui optimizes offices, retail stores, restaurants, and corporate buildings to attract prosperity, enhance productivity, and support business growth.

We analyze customer flow patterns, employee work areas, meeting rooms, and entrance positioning. The goal is to create an environment that attracts clients, motivates staff, and facilitates successful transactions and partnerships.

Business Applications

  • Office Layout: Maximizing productivity and harmony
  • CEO Desk Position: Command position for leadership
  • Reception Area: First impression and client attraction
  • Meeting Rooms: Successful negotiations and decisions
  • Retail Store Design: Customer flow and sales optimization
  • Restaurant Seating: Atmosphere and table turnover
  • Signage & Branding: Visibility and business identity
  • Wealth Corners: Activating prosperity sectors
